Estrad's Black Board
Estrad’s Blackboard is a website which instantaneously enhances interactions between members and organizations and it offers personalized services and attention such as email, chats, forums, calendars, news and also customized information such virtual class room for education industries, project management for IT industries. Estrad’s Black Board can provide these capabilities across the world by unifying enterprise applications and information through a standard web browser. It can be used for any kind of enterprise (Education, IT, medical, etc).
