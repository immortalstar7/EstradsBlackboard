Frequently asked Questions

What is Estrad’s BlackBoard? »

Estrad’s BlackBoard is basically a no-cost online learning environment that you can set up to match your personal teaching style, curricula, and student needs. It can give you significant power beyond the traditional classroom. Estrad’s BlackBoard is available to you anytime, 24/7 via the Internet.

How much does Estrad’s BlackBoards cost? »

We are serious, Estrad’s BlackBoard is absolutely FREE.

How do I get started? »

Getting started is simple. Sign up by the following three steps below. 1. Click the Sign Up Now link at the top of this web page.
2. Login using a current Facebook, Twitter, Gmail, Hotmail, or Yahoo username and password, or create a new account in Estrad’s BlackBoard.
3. Agree to the Terms of Use.

What if I forget my password? »

No problem. We’ve tried to keep that from happening by allowing you to login using your Facebook, Twitter, Gmail, Hotmail, or Yahoo account information. If you don’t have one of those accounts, no worries. Use the “Forget Your Password?” link on the login page to quickly change your password.

Is Estrad’s BlackBoards secure? »

Absolutely. Estrad’s BlackBoard is a password protected site which requires all users to login using a current Facebook, Twitter, Hotmail, Gmail or Yahoo account, or a chosen Estrad’s BlackBoard username and password.

What types of files can I upload to Estrad’s BlackBoard? »

Instructors and learners can upload FOLLOWING file types to a Estrad’s BlackBoard:
TEXT FILES: PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT
PRESENTATION: PPT, PPTX
VIDEO FILES: AVI, MP4, 3GP
